Fair to creators

We don't break the blogs you love.

Recipe blogs are how a lot of people make a living — and a lot of those recipes are great because the people writing them care. Pantry helps you find the part you came for. It doesn't take anything away from the people who made it.

If you love a blog, keep visiting it. We just make it less of a slog.

  • ✕ No stripping ads. The page loads exactly as the creator built it. Ads run normally.
  • ✕ No extracting recipes. We never lift the recipe off the page or republish it elsewhere.
  • ✓ Just scroll & augment. We add tools on top of the page — a scroll, a tooltip, a notes panel. Nothing else.
  • ✓ The author still gets paid. Their work, their traffic, their ad revenue — all intact.

How it works

Four steps. No setup.

Install Pantry

Add it from the Chrome Web Store and open any recipe blog. There's nothing to configure.

Land on the recipe

The page auto-scrolls straight to the recipe — no clicking "Jump to Recipe."

Bookmark or note it

Click the toolbar icon to save it, or open "My Notes" to jot down your tweaks.

Come back anytime

Your bookmarks and notes are right where you left them, on every device signed into Chrome.
